About Me

My name is Mark Howe. I am an owner / operator of a custom cabinet manufacturing facility out of North Georgia. Throughout the years I have honed my skills as a business owner facing the daily challenges of entrepreneurship. Even though I have enjoyed the opportunity to serve others through my business I find myself in search of something more. I have come to the conclusion that although I create beautiful furniture grade cabinets for my clients I would not change the world for the better with the skills utilized in custom cabinet fabrication.
