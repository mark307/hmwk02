About Me

My name is Mark Howe. I am an owner / operator of a custom cabinet manufacturing facility located in North Georgia. Throughout the years I have honed my skills as a business owner facing the daily challenges of entrepreneurship. Even though I have enjoyed the opportunity to serve others through my business I find myself in search of something more. I have come to the conclusion that although I create beautiful furniture grade cabinets for my customers I would not be able not change the world for the better with the skills utilized in custom cabinet fabrication.

Understanding that technology is the driving force that ensures the modern world turns I made the decision to embark on a new journey and pursue a career in the field of computer programming. After researching a plethora of options for education I found a program offered at Georgia Tech which was the best fit and has amazing results for its students. I enrolled immediately to ensure that I secured a placement in the poplar coarse and now I am happy to say that it is one of the better choices that I have made in my professional career.

I live in the city of Cartersville, Georgia, which is where is graduated from Cartersville High School. I have a wonderful wife and 4 children, and I hope to teach my children what I learn as a developer so that they may be prepared for their future paths in life.
